Connect Your TikTok Account

Authorize GHOST M to publish videos to your TikTok account using TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 login flow.

  1. Click the button below to open TikTok's secure login page
  2. Log in with your TikTok account credentials
  3. Review and approve the permissions requested
  4. You'll be redirected back here — GHOST M will begin publishing automatically
Login with TikTok

Permissions requested: read profile info, upload and publish videos. No passwords are stored.

TikTok Integration

GHOST M uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish original short-form videos to creator accounts. All content is original and complies with TikTok's Community Guidelines.

The platform uses OAuth 2.0 via TikTok Login Kit to securely connect accounts. No passwords are stored — only access tokens provided by TikTok's authorization flow.
