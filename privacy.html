Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GHOST M collects, uses, and protects information in connection with your use of our automated content publishing platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize GHOST M to connect to your TikTok account, we receive and store:

  • TikTok OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by TikTok authorization
  • Your TikTok user ID and basic profile information (display name, avatar URL)
  • Video upload history and performance metrics from your TikTok account

We do not collect your TikTok password or any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used exclusively to authenticate API requests to publish videos, retrieve performance analytics, and display account status. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored locally on secured systems and are never transmitted to external servers beyond what is required for TikTok API calls.

4. TikTok API Data Usage

GHOST M uses the TikTok Content Posting API and Login Kit with scopes: video.publish, video.upload, user.info.basic, user.info.stats, and video.list. We comply with TikTok API Terms of Service and do not use data to train AI/ML models.

5. Data Retention and Your Rights

You may revoke TikTok access at any time via TikTok account settings under Manage App Permissions. You may also request data deletion by contacting us.
